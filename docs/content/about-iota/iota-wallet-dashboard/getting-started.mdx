The IOTA Wallet  Dashboard offers an easy-to-use platform for managing your digital assets on the IOTA network.

- [Send](./how-to/basics.mdx#send-coins) and [Receive](./how-to/basics.mdx#receive-coins) IOTA
- [Check Your Wallet Balance](./how-to/basics.mdx#assets-and-activity-overview)
- [Review Your Recent Activity](./how-to/basics.mdx#assets-and-activity-overview)
- [Manage Your Assets](./how-to/assets.mdx)
- [Stake Your Tokens](./how-to/stake.mdx)
- [View](./how-to/vesting.mdx#view-currently-vested-tokens), [Collect](./how-to/vesting.mdx#collect-vesting-rewards) and [Stake](./how-to/vesting.mdx#stake-vested-tokens) Vested Tokens
- [Migrate Your Wallet's Assets](./how-to/migration.mdx)

## Connect Your Wallet

To connect your IOTA Wallet to the Dashboard, click on the `Connect Wallet` button.

   ![Connect button](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet-dashboard/getting-started/connect-wallet.png)

And then select the wallet you want to connect to.

   ![Select Wallet](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet-dashboard/getting-started/select-wallet.png)

### Select a Network

You can use the gear icon on the top right to access the settings menu.

   ![Gear Icon](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet-dashboard/getting-started/gear-icon.png)

Once you are in the settings menu, select `Network`.

   ![Click Network](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet-dashboard/getting-started/click-network.png)

You should now see a list of the available networks for you to choose.

   ![Select Your Network](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet-dashboard/getting-started/select-network.png)
