# Send Funds From L1 to L2

This guide will show you how to send funds from L1 to L2 using the [IOTA EVM Bridge](https://evm-bridge.iota.org/).

## Prerequisites

- [Metamask wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/metamask/nkbihfbeogaeaoehlefnkodbefgpgknn?hl=en)
- [IOTA wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/iota-wallet/iidjkmdceolghepehaaddojmnjnkkija?hl=en)

## Steps

1. Open the [IOTA EVM Bridge](https://evm-bridge.iota.org/).

2. Connect your IOTA wallet.

    ![Connect your IOTA wallet](/img/iota-evm/evm/how-tos/get-funds/connect-iota-wallet.png)

3. Connect your Metamask wallet by clicking on the `Connect Wallet` button.
    
    ![Connect your Metamask wallet](/img/iota-evm/evm/how-tos/get-funds/connect-metamask.png)

4. Enter the amount of iota coins you want to transfer to L2. You can also request funds from the faucet by clicking on the `Request Funds` button.

    ![Transfer funds from L1 to L2](/img/iota-evm/evm/how-tos/get-funds/enter-iota-amount.png)

5. Click on the bridger assets button to transfer the funds.

    ![Send Transaction](/img/iota-evm/evm/how-tos/get-funds/approve-transaction.png)

You can now check the eth balance in your metamask wallet.
